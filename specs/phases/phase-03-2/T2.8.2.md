### Technical Direction

- **Text samples**: 
  - ASCII: "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog" (all sizes)
  - Latin Extended: "Ünïcödé tëst àçcéñts" (24px)
  - CJK: "中文渲染测试 日本語テスト" (24px, 48px)
  - Arabic: "مرحبا بالعالم" (24px, RTL)
  - Engineering: "Ø12.5 ±0.01 45° ⊥ ∥" (24px)
  - Rich text: "Normal **bold** *italic* ^super^ ~sub~" (24px)
- **Size comparison panel**: Grid of the same string at 6, 8, 12, 16, 24, 48, 72 px. Below 8px shows bitmap fallback; above shows MSDF. **Note**: Arabic sample "مرحبا بالعالم" rendered on a separate line (single-direction RTL) — Phase 2 does not support paragraph-level BiDi mixing (deferred to Phase 7b FriBidi). Mixed Latin+Arabic in one line may display incorrectly.
- **World-space labels**: 3D scene with a few cubes, each with a billboarded label.
- **ImGui debug panel**: Atlas page count, resident glyph count, cache hit rate, draw call count, instance count.
