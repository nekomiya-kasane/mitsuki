### Technical Direction

- **Hash dedup**: FNV-1a hash over `sizeof(StandardPBR)` bytes. `std::unordered_map<uint64_t, MaterialId>` for dedup. Flat `std::vector<StandardPBR>` for storage (indexed by `MaterialId`).

## Design Decisions

- **Hash over MaterialParameterBlock, not StandardPBR**: FNV-1a operates on `ToParameterBlock()` output (64 bytes, explicit padding, `static_assert(sizeof==64)`) instead of raw `StandardPBR` bytes. This avoids padding-dependent hash mismatches caused by compiler struct layout differences.
- **PresetTable as pointer+size**: Avoided `std::span` due to coca toolchain `<span>` availability issues. Used a simple `PresetTable{const PresetDef* data, size_t count}` struct instead.
- **23 presets**: 9 metals + 4 plastics + 3 glass + 2 rubber + 2 ceramics + 1 composite + 2 wood. All values sourced from measured BRDF databases and Filament material guide.
