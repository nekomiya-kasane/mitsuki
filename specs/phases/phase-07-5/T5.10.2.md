### Technical Direction

- **TopoGraphComponent**: ECS component per-entity containing:
  - `TopoGraph graph` — the body's topology
  - `std::vector<FaceId> triangleToFaceMap` — indexed by global triangle index → FaceId
  - `std::vector<EdgePolyline> edgePolylines` — 3D polyline per B-Rep edge
- **PopulateFromTessellation**: `(Entity, TessellationResult const&, ComponentRegistry&) -> void` — attaches `TopoGraphComponent` to entity, fills topology from tessellation result.
- **PrimitiveToFace**: `TopoGraphComponent::PrimitiveToFace(uint32_t globalTriIdx) -> FaceId` — O(1) lookup via `triangleToFaceMap[triIdx]`. Used by Phase 7a-2 picking.
- **Serialization placeholder**: `.miki` archive stores `triangleToFaceMap` + `FaceType` per face alongside meshlet data (Phase 6b).
