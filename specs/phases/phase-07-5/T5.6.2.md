### Technical Direction

- **Uniform grid**: world-space divided into cells of configurable size. Hash function: `hash(ix, iy, iz) = (ix * 73856093) ^ (iy * 19349669) ^ (iz * 83492791)` (standard spatial hash primes).
- **Open addressing**: `std::unordered_map<uint64_t, std::vector<uint32_t>>` for simplicity. Phase 14 can optimize to flat hash map if bottleneck.
- **No world bounds**: unbounded — any position is valid (unlike Octree which requires root AABB).
