## Scope

Full CAD interaction demo integrating all Phase 7a-2 components:

- **Scene**: STEP assembly (or procedural) with opaque + transparent + X-Ray objects.
- **OIT**: Linked-list OIT for transparent objects, X-Ray mode toggle.
- **Picking**: All 6 modes (point/box/lasso × no-drill/drill). SelectionFilter UI.
- **Explode**: Multi-level explode with smoothstep animation.
- **Lighting**: 100+ point/spot lights via clustered culling. Shadow atlas for shadow-casting lights.
- **Post-process**: SSR on reflective surfaces, DoF in Realistic mode, motion blur during turntable, CAS + color grading.
- **RTAO**: Toggle RTAO vs GTAO.
- **ImGui**: Pick mode selector, explode slider, light count display, OIT layer stats, shadow atlas utilization, per-pass GPU timing.
- **CLI**: `--backend vulkan --lights 100 --display-style Realistic`
