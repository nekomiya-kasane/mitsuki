## Scope

Per arch spec §7.3 Pass #14:
- **ShadowAtlas**: Single D32F texture atlas for point/spot/area light shadows.
- **Tier1**: 8192² atlas. Point=512²/face (6 faces = 6 tiles), spot=1024², area=1024².
- **Tier2/3/4**: 4096² atlas. Point=256², spot=512².
- **LRU tile management**: Each shadow-casting light claims tiles. When atlas full, evict least-recently-used light's tiles. Max 32 shadow-casting lights/frame (T1), 8 (T2/3/4).
- **Distance-based resolution**: Lights farther from camera get lower-res tiles. Scaling: `tileRes = baseRes * clamp(1.0 / distance, 0.25, 1.0)`.
- **`GpuLight.shadowIndex`**: Index into atlas tile table. `0xFFFFFFFF` = no shadow.
