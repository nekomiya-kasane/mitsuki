## Scope

Linearly Transformed Cosines (LTC) lookup textures for physically-correct area light specular evaluation. Per arch spec §7.1: "LTC lookup textures (2× RGBA32F 64×64, 128KB) for area light specular."

- **LTC_LUT1** (RGBA32F 64×64): inverse matrix coefficients M^-1 indexed by (roughness, cos_theta).
- **LTC_LUT2** (RGBA32F 64×64): magnitude/Fresnel coefficients indexed by (roughness, cos_theta).
- **Generation**: One-time compute at init from precomputed data (Heitz et al. 2016 "Real-Time Polygonal-Light Shading with Linearly Transformed Cosines").
- **Area light types**: AreaRect (4-vertex polygon), AreaDisc (projected to polygon), AreaTube (2-end-cap rectangles).
- **Integration**: Deferred resolve reads LTC textures when evaluating `GpuLight.type == AreaRect/AreaDisc/AreaTube`.
